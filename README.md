# INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE
This is the control panel for the Traffic Light Simulation.



<img width="306" alt="screen shot 2018-09-07 at 8 12 14 am" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/13387177/45227627-c755b200-b275-11e8-9070-a8ab3bfc7f42.png">
By hitting the "Step Animation" button, you can observe the animation of a normal stop light.

To observe a left turn signal, press the "Left Please" on either the north-south or east-west traffic, and continue the step animation.
To observe the cross walk in effect, press the "Cross Please" button.
